Home Builder

In this section we always try to find a new way that can help you when you build your home or redesign your home.

Paints Maker

Paints Maker is a tool where you can find and choose different types of colors for your home interior and exterior. These colors names and their code numbers are based on Asian Paints Company. You can choose any type of color and note the color code and name and go to your nearest Asian Paints dealer and get your perfect combination color.

Tiles Calculator

This tool helps you to calculate how many tiles and marbles you want. There are many different types of tile and marble sizes.

  1. Measure your walls or floors.
  2. Select a size.
  3. Click the calculate button.

Here you can see the total numbers of your requirement.

Workers Finder

Shop Together

Suppose you purchase metal stone, steel, and cement together for your home construction work; you pay the materials value with a transporting charge that is normally, but when you purchase a minimum quantity of materials, that time you also pay the same transporting charge.

This tool is to help you to lower the transporting charge when you purchase the minimum quantity of materials.

How does it work?

Suppose you want some materials for your home construction work. and another one person in the same situation also wants some materials for her work. and both locations are in the same direction. Then you can use this tool.

Both will be providing their materials names with quantities and their location.We will be combining those materials in one vehicle and dividing the transporting charge with each other. Then your transporting charge will be low.
